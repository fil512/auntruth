Source Meadows MB Centennial Project History Book 1967
Notes When Andrew was 20 he was brought to Winnipeg by his eldest brother Borje. Andrew worked for Borje delivering water, selling it by the pail to city hotels and restaurants, hauling it from artesian wells northwest of the city. In 1895 he married Johanna Wall who came to Winnipeg when she was 16 and worked at Jerry Robinson's home. After their marriage Andrew worked at Arctic Ice Co. and Johanna kept a boarding house. In 1899 they bought a farm and moved to Starbuck. Andrew continued to work at Arctic Ice in the winter. In 1903 they bought 240 acres at Meadows from Mr. Osterberg. They moved to Meadows in 1903 via horse and buggy. Andrew died of pneumonia in 1905 while Johanna was pregnant with Andy. Johanna married employee Victor Carlson in 1907.
