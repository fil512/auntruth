Notes Immigrated with wife Wilhelmine from Russia in 1892. bringing August, Paulina, Mary, Theophilia; settling first in Winnipeg. In 1896 August Selch took a homestead on 16-13-8E in Green Bay, MB. Christian settled with them. The homestead was a dense forest. With the help of a team of oxen they began to clear the land. Soon they also acquired a horse. The horse, teamed up with the one owned by the neighbouring Litke family enabled the 2 families to make trips to Beausejour for supplies. German records show Selch name also Salcher, Selchert
