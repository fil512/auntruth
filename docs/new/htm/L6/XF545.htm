NotesGraduated Electrical Engineer from University of Manitoba in about 1973. After working in larger organisations, became technical operations director for Computerland Western Canada franchise. Had had a SOL microcomputer prior to 1977. Was introduced to Connie Selch by David & Lorraine Hagborg summer 1979 when David was developing software for Computerland customers. The meeting took place at David's parents' cottage at Caddy Lake MB when the stars were especially brilliant in the dark, non-urban sky.
