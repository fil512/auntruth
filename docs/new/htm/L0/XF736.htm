Source Meadows MB Centennial Project History Book 1967
Notes Attended school at Meadows and Winnipeg. Obtained teachers certificate in 1941. Taught 2 years at Westerham School in Teulon district and 3 years at Woodlands. Married Jim Rothwell of Woodlands in 1947. Lived in Winnipeg then bought the general store at Meadows from Mr. Dowler in 1949. Discontinued the business in 1967. Irene returned to teaching in 1962 and taught in St. James-Assiniboia, Winnipeg.
