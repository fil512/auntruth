NotesJoined Canadian Navy in 1942, stationed in Halifax/Sidney NS and St. John NF. Was educated in Winnipeg, and while studying accounting at business college he joined Hagborg Fuel. In 1954 he became a manufacturers agent representing firms in Canada, the United States, and England. As a young man Don was member of the Order of the Demolay, Winnipeg Junior Chamber of Commerce and Kildonan Canoe Club. Don was a member of the board of the Central YMCA and a longtime member of the Northwest Commercial Travellers. Don was an active Mason, first with the Fort Garry Lodge, then as a founding member of the Gateway Lodge. He later joined the Friendship Lodge and was Master in 1956. He was also a member of the Scottish Rite Bethesda Chapter Rose Croix. He was a member of the Granite Curling Club and the Wildewood Club where he enjoyed many years of curling and golf. Don enjoyed Toastmasters 250 for several years, and won a number of speech contests.
