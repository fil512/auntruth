NotesHave picture when a young boy As a member of the Engineering Institute of Canada he held positions as Chief Engineer for Vulcan Iron and Engineering and Bridge and Tank Co. Winnipeg. In 1962 he moved to Victoria BC and at the time of his retirement he was General Manager of Victoria Machinery Depot Co. In his active years he took part in the development of codes and standards for the Canadian Standards Association and the Canadian Institute of Steel Construction. Buried in Elmwood Cemetery Winnipeg.
