NotesWas one of the founders of St. Paul's Lutheran Church in Green Bay, MB 1898. Married Amalia Weise 04/20/1900 who arrived from Europe. They met and were married in Winnipeg by Pastor F. Beer. In 1917 they moved to a farm on the east side of Beausejour, but the Green Bay property was kept for grain. They started a dairy business in 1928. Initially all the cows were milked by hand, and the milk, in 8 gallon cans had to be hauled by horse-drawn wagon to the railway station in Beausejour, to be shipped to Winnipeg. By 1935 the milk was picked up by a transfer truck and hauled to the city. August Selch was the first to own a cream separator.
