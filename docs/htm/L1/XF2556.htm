NotesChristina Sofia was born APril 26, 1902 in Winnipeg. She died May 17, 1903 and is buried in Brookside Cemetary, Sec 21, Lot 50. Christina and Clarence both contacted diptheria. Johanna and Andrew took both infants to Winnipeg, but there was no room in the hospitals for them. They took the children to a friends house where the children could stay in the attic. Clarence recovered from the illness but Christina died. When they took the children into Winnipeg, they left at home 6 year old Victor and 3 year old Harry for 2-3 days alone.
