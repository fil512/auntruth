NotesWhen father Isaac died, he went to libe with his grandfather William Pringle. He was close with Lemon. William worked for a dairy in Johnstown and later went to Ohio and from there to Pensacola FL where he died and is buried. He went to Canada in 1911 with brother Lemon to visit Ira and Franklin. The day they arrived, Ira was cutting wheat, and that night Ella gave birth to Walter. The visit was timely as William helped Ira on the farm and Lemon took over the housework, cooking & the care of little Margaret.
